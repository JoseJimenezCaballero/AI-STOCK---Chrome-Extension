AI STOCK

AI-Driven Stock Predictor

For Yahoo Finance

AI STOCK will automatically detect the stock you are looking at on Yahoo Finance. It will then train a Linear Regression machine learning model on the latest data to predict the stock's future movement. The extension displays these predictions, indicating whether to buy, sell, or hold the stock, helping you make informed investment decisions. FOR EDUCATIONAL PURPOSES ONLY
